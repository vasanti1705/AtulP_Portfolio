About

I am an assistant professor at Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering, with 11 years of teaching experience. My main interests lie in Image Processing, Wireless Sensor Networks, Internet of Things, and Computer Networks. I strongly believe in the power of experiential learning and reflection for student development. I strive to create environments that foster meaningful learning experiences. Since 2017, I have been entrusted with the responsibility of overseeing PCCOE's art circle committee. I take great pride in guiding and mentoring the students, who have achieved remarkable success, winning numerous titles in renowned competitions. In pursuit of continuous professional growth, I actively participate in various technical workshops and faculty development programs held at different colleges.

  • July 2015

  • Summary
